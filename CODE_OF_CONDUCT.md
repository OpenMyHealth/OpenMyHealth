# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in openChart respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on code and outcomes.
- Protect user privacy and sensitive health information.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Sharing personal health data from issues or logs.
- Publishing exploit details without coordinated disclosure.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this policy.
